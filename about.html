What is SelectricGo?

SelectricGO is EV startup with a vision to make India #1 in Electric Vehicles adoption.We are a group of like minded,highly passionate people with a zeal to fight the climate changes happens due to utilisation of fossil fuels. As the most advanced creation of god, we should understand the importance and the need to save our planet Earth, we should understand that we have not inherited these resources from our ancestors instead borrowed them from our future generations. So being a responsible citizen it is our duty to contribute. Thus we founded SelectricGO with a motive to increase EV penetration rate in India. "EV for all" sounds interesting right ?

Even to us. Thus SelectricGO brings you easy solutions to your little problems. SelectricGO provides you the information regarding charging stations near your location and much more.
